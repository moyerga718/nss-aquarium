Tank tips

Water Temperature

These are some picky fish! Gotta keep em nice and warm.

Water Salinity

My babies are from the salty salty sea. I tried to bring ocean water on the plane back from Indonesia, but they made me empty out my water bottles at security... darn TSA!

Maybe you won't get that real ocean water smell (death/rot/oil/trash) by doing this, but you can get a hose, a metric ton of salt, and mix that up in a bucket. No measurements needed.

Cleanliness

Now the other websites will recommend against this (trust me, I've been shot down on every fish forum on the web), but the tank won't get dirty if you keep the clorox levels in the water just right! I recommend a 70/30 ratio, clorox to salt water.
